We are centered around a holistic athletic program in sports.

We are centered around a holistic athletic program in sports for the advancement and development of all participants in all sports. We are focused on the improvement of youngsters of all capacities in a variety of sporting activities. Our activities also focus on participants personal development in areas of self-confidence, good attitude, discipline, team work and hard work.

Additionally, LKO Foundation also organise annual foreign sports trips. We are also into construction and renovation of sports facilities, organization of sports competitions, establishment of sports academy in schools such as football academy, basketball club, organization of football camp, basketball camp, etc.
